Niall Horan


Niall James Horan[1] (born September 13, 1993) is a member of One Direction along
with Harry Styles, Liam Payne, Louis Tomlinson and ex-member Zayn Malik. He is the only Irish member of the band.
He is currently signed to Capitol Records as a solo artist but remains a member of One Direction.
Niall released his debut solo single This Town on 29 September, 2016, and released his debut album, Flicker
on 20 October 2017. His second solo album "Heartbreak Weather" was released on 13 March 2020.

Liam Payne


Liam James Payne (born August 29, 1993) was a member of One Direction along
with Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and Niall Horan.
Liam released his debut single, Strip That Down, on 19 May, 2017. His debut EP, First Time, was released on August 24, 2018.
His debut album, LP1, was released on 6 December, 2019. Liam remixed One Direction's "You & I" and
"Steal My Girl". Payne and Tomlinson were named in 2015 as some of Song Splits Top Songwriters in the World. The
annual list showcases who in the entertainment industry is the most relevant and valuable, based on airplay, digital sales and web streams.

Louis Tomlinson


Louis William Tomlinson (born Louis Troy Austin, December 24, 1991) is a member of
One Direction along with Harry Styles, Liam Payne, and Niall Horan and former member Zayn Malik.
He is the oldest member of the group. From 2017-2018, Louis was signed as a solo artist to
Syco and Epic Records. In 2019, Louis transitioned to Arista Records. Louis released his debut solo single,
Just Hold On, on December 10, 2016. His debut album, Walls, was released on 31 January, 2020.
